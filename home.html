About us

M.G. Nlong Nig. Limited is an indigenous company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the year 2025. Though a newly registered company, M.G. Nlong Nig. Limited has been in existence for a while, operating and rendering car hire services.

M.G. Nlong Nig. Limited is a trusted provider of premium services in our areas of competence, meeting the needs of our clients with a commitment to quality, flexibility and client satisfaction in a simple, affordable and convenient way.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional services that prioritize client satisfaction, operational excellence and uncompromising safety in our areas of competence. We are committed to delivering flexible, reliable and high quality mobility solutions that meet the diverse needs of individual and corporate clients.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading service provider in our areas of core competence, recognized for our integrity, innovation and unwavering dedication to customer care. We aim to set industry benchmarks for service excellence and expand our reach across domestic and international markets.

Core value

Commitment to excellence — delivering superior service quality at every stage. Integrity — upholding transparency, trust and ethical practices. Client-centric approach — prioritizing the needs and satisfaction of every client.

Meet the Founder


What began as a handful of trusted drivers ferrying friends and colleagues between Jos and Abuja has grown into a registered, multi‑branch transport company. The idea was simple: Nigerians deserve a car hire service that shows up on time, prices honestly, and treats every passenger like family. Today that same standard runs through every branch — Jos, Abuja, Bauchi and Gombe — because a good journey shouldn't depend on which city you're travelling from.
